Our Facility Is An Impressive Classic Industrial Warehouse

Our facility is an impressive classic industrial warehouse event aesthetics with modern artistic sensibilities. The arena is unique with over 10,000+ square feet that provides ample room for the best possible indoor futsal experience.

Featuring Graffiti Art From World Renown Artists

Internationally known artists from LA and around the world showcase their work within our unique warehouse canvas. Rotating original pieces serve as the backdrop for a time until replaced by new artwork.

Mixing Sound, Art, And Technology

Play futsal with friends while enjoying street art, music, and social media. Emerging DJs have been known to drop in and spin music on select nights. Share your picture and video highlights from the game taken at multiple angles.
